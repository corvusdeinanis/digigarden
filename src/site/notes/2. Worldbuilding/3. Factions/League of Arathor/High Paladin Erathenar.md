# History

Erathenar led his people to many great victories against the [[2. Worldbuilding/3. Factions/Defilers/Defilers\|Defilers]], earning them respect throughout [[2. Worldbuilding/2. But with courage and determination, Erathenar has overcome even these obstacles and is now widely respected for his leadership and bravery in battle.
